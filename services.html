Our Services

Welcome to the website of the cabinet of psychologists! We are a team of experienced and passionate psychologists who are dedicated to providing quality therapy services to adults, couples, families and young people. We are here to provide you with a safe and confidential environment so that you can discuss your concerns with confidence. Our mission is to help people overcome their difficulties to improve their psychological well-being. We tailor our approach to meet each individual's needs and goals to maximize positive therapy outcomes.

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy provides a safe environment to explore what's bothering you at work, in your relationships, or about yourself. In this process, you and your psychotherapist will form a team and work together to find solutions that will have an impact on your life.

Consultation Price : 125$

Couple Therapy

Couple therapy is a process undertaken by partners wishing to overcome the difficulties of living together in an impartial environment. Our experts are there to help partners who want to improve their life as a couple.

Consultation Price : 205$

Familial Therapy

Many reasons can affect the family balance. Most of the time, the suffering of one family member masks a dysfunction in the group as a whole. Family therapy is there to deal with these difficulties and improve family cohesion.

Consultation Price : 300$
